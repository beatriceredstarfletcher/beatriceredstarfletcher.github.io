Beatrice Red Star Fletcher > About

Beatrice Red Star Fletcher works across mediums with an extensive background in drawing, sewing, and comedy. Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, Red Star Fletcher began her collaborative work with her father, Harrell Fletcher, when she was three years old, and her mother, Wendy Red Star, at age seven in her first collaborative exhibition Medicine Crow & The 1880 Crow Peace Delegation at the Portland Art Museum. She continues to work with both parents and on various solo projects as well.
